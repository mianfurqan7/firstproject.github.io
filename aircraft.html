› FLARIS & LAR 01 ‹
The first and only aircraft of its type in the world, FLARIS LAR 01 unites jet-tier performance with levels of safety and economic accessibility previously reserved solely for small general aviation units. The cutting-edge construction made overcoming many traditional limitations possible and resulted in creation of an internationally exceptional aircraft!
› FEATURES ‹
FLARIS LAR 1 is a single-engine jet aircraft with lightweight, composite construction. It
creates a new category of small, agile jets, ideal for quick movement for any purpose.
SOAR TO A HIGHER LEVEL OF COMFORT
The intuition of piloting FLARIS LAR 1 is apparent in its minimal operations, secure stall, and automatic spin recovery. The jet was designed to be flown by a single pilot (with Garmin G600 Txi radio and navigation system and FFAS® interactive onboard computer acting as a copilot). The only qualification required to fly FLARIS LAR 1 is a Private Pilot License (PPL).
